Bukit Besar


Bukit Besar is one of the famous tourist destinations in Kuala Terengganu. From the top of this hill, you can see all corners of Kuala Terengganu and the beautiful panorama of the sea from a height.

  • One of the iconic features of this hill is the inscription 'Allah Peliharakanlah Terengganu' which lights up at night.
  • If you visit this hill, don't forget to bring drinking water so you don't get thirsty.
  • Don't forget to capture your ascent by taking pictures.

Jalan TM Bukit Besar, 21100 Kuala Terengganu, Malaysia

Bukit Balik Hidung


Bukit Balik Hidung was popularized by the climbing association at the beginning of 2020 and until now this hill has become famous and has not stopped with its visitors.

  • The hill, which is 132 meters above sea level, is suitable for all walks of life, whether children or adults, because it doesn't take long to reach the top.
  • Although the hill is not that high and has an easy path, you also need to warm up before starting to climb.
  • This is to prevent you from getting injured during hill climbing activities.
  • The view of green trees from the top of this hill will definitely make you not regret climbing Bukit Balik Hidung.

Kampung Payar Besar, 21700 Kuala Berang, Terengganu, Malaysia


24 Hours

Bukit Maras


Bukit Maras which is located in Kuala Nerus district, only takes about 30 minutes to drive if you are from the city of Kuala Terengganu.

  • With a height of 329 meters above sea level, Bukit Maras also has no shortage of people who come here to climb.
  • Unlike Bukit Besar, Bukit Maras has 3 routes you can choose to reach the top.

21020 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu, Malaysia


24 Hours

Bukit Tok Salai


Bukit Tok Salai which is located on the Nerus River, has just opened to the public in September 2021 and has become an attraction for hiking enthusiasts.

  • This hill, which is 160 meters above sea level, only takes about 30 to 40 minutes to reach the top.
  • The Tok Salai Hill Management in collaboration with the Buluh Gading Assemblyman take great care of cleanliness and also provide facilities on this hill for the comfort of the climbers.
  • Among the basic facilities that have been provided on the hill are waqaf rehat, surau and also toilets for the use of visitors. With the facilities available, it will certainly attract more visitors to come to Bukit Tok Salai.

    • Bukit Tok Salai Sungai Nerus, 21200 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu, Malaysia
