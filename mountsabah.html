Mount Kinabalu


The highest peak in Southeast Asia, Mount Kinabalu is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most famous trekking destinations. The summit provides breathtaking views of the surrounding area, and the diverse flora and fauna along the trail make it a must-visit for nature enthusiasts.

  • Mount Kinabalu is the highest peak in Southeast Asia, offering stunning vistas at its summit.
  • It is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site, attracting trekkers and nature lovers from around the world.
  • The mountain features diverse flora and fauna, making the journey to the summit an exceptional experience for nature enthusiasts.

Kinabalu Park, near Kota Kinabalu.


Mount Trus Madi


The second-highest peak in Sabah, Mount Trus Madi offers a challenging trek through dense forests. The journey provides a serene escape into nature, with opportunities for wildlife spotting and experiencing Sabah's biodiversity.

  • Mount Trus Madi is the second-highest peak in Sabah, known for its challenging trek through dense forests.
  • The mountain offers a serene escape into nature, making it ideal for those seeking adventure and tranquility.
  • Wildlife spotting and experiencing Sabah's biodiversity are key highlights during the trek.

Between Ranau and Keningau.


Mount Tambuyukon


Known for its rugged terrain and dense forests, Mount Tambuyukon is less crowded than Mount Kinabalu, making it perfect for those seeking a quieter and more remote adventure.

  • Mount Tambuyukon is famous for its rugged terrain and dense forests.
  • It is less crowded than Mount Kinabalu, offering a more secluded and peaceful adventure.
  • This mountain is perfect for those seeking a quiet, remote experience in nature.

Kinabalu Park, near Mount Kinabalu


Mount Alab


Mount Alab is known for its cooler climate, scenic views, and diverse wildlife. It is a great spot for those looking to escape the heat and enjoy nature.

  • Mount Alab offers a cooler climate compared to lower altitudes.
  • The mountain is famous for its scenic views and diverse wildlife.
  • It is an excellent destination for those looking to escape the heat and enjoy nature.

Near the town of Tambunan
