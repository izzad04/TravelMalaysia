Armenian Street


Armenian Street, or Lebuh Armenian, is a vibrant and historic street located in the heart of George Town, Penang. Known for its rich cultural heritage and artistic charm, the street offers a unique experience for visitors.

  • Street Art: Features colorful murals, including the iconic "Children on a Bicycle" by Ernest Zacharevic.
  • Local Attractions: Explore quaint shophouses, galleries, antique stores, and cafes.
  • Traditional Trades: Witness joss stick making and other heritage crafts.
  • Cuisine: Sample a variety of local delicacies reflecting Penang's multicultural history.
  • Must-Visit: Ideal for culture, art, and history enthusiasts.

ESCAPE Penang


ESCAPE Penang is an award-winning adventure and theme park located in Teluk Bahang, offering an eco-friendly and thrilling experience for visitors of all ages.

  • Activities: Includes zip-lining, obstacle courses, water slides, and the world’s longest tube water slide.
  • Eco-Friendly Concept: The park is designed to promote fun and physical activity in a natural setting surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Suitability: Perfect for adrenaline seekers and family-friendly entertainment.
  • Memorable Experience: ESCAPE Penang guarantees an unforgettable adventure for everyone.

The Top Komtar


Top Komtar is a prominent shopping and entertainment complex located in the heart of George Town, Penang, known for its modern retail experiences and breathtaking views.

  • Location: Centrally located in George Town, Penang.
  • Height: One of the tallest buildings in the region.
  • Observation Deck: Situated on the 65th floor, offering panoramic views of Penang's cityscape and coastline.
  • Komtar Mall: Features a variety of shops, restaurants, and attractions, including an indoor amusement park.
  • Purpose: A hub for sightseeing, shopping, and leisure activities.

Jalan Penang, George Town, Penang

Tropical Spice Garden


The Tropical Spice Garden is a lush botanical oasis located in Teluk Bahang, Penang, spanning over 8 acres and featuring a diverse collection of tropical plants, spices, and herbs.

  • Themed Gardens: Includes the Spice Garden, Herb Garden, and Butterfly Garden.
  • Activities: Offers guided tours, cooking classes, and nature walks.
  • Focus: Learn about the history, culinary, and medicinal uses of various plants.
  • Experience: A serene atmosphere ideal for nature lovers and educational exploration.

Lot 595, Jalan Teluk Bahang, Penang


Every day

9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
