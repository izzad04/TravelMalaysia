Tanjung Aru Beach


Renowned for its stunning sunsets, Tanjung Aru Beach is a favorite for relaxing strolls, picnics, and enjoying the view of the South China Sea. The beach is family-friendly, with nearby food stalls and amenities.

  • Famous for its breathtaking sunsets, making it a top spot for evening views.
  • Ideal for relaxing strolls along the shoreline and peaceful picnics with a view of the South China Sea.
  • Family-friendly with accessible facilities, including food stalls and other amenities.
  • Offers a tranquil escape within proximity to the city of Kota Kinabalu.

Tanjung Aru Beach Resort, Tanjung Aru, 88100 Kota Kinabalu, Sabah

Dalit Beach


This serene and luxurious beach is part of the Shangri-La Rasa Ria Resort. It offers a peaceful retreat with golden sands and opportunities for watersports, perfect for those seeking relaxation.

  • Located within the Shangri-La Rasa Ria Resort, offering a tranquil and exclusive beach experience.
  • Golden sandy shores ideal for relaxation and leisurely walks along the beach.
  • Provides opportunities for various watersports, making it perfect for both relaxation and adventure.
  • Perfect for those seeking a luxurious and serene environment to unwind and enjoy the coastal beauty.

Dalit Beach, 89200 Tuaran, Sabah

24 Hours

Bak Bak Beach


Known for its rocky outcrops and stunning views, Bak Bak Beach offers a serene environment for picnicking and enjoying the coastal breeze. It's a peaceful spot to unwind, far from crowded tourist areas.

  • Famous for its unique rocky outcrops, providing a scenic backdrop to the beach.
  • Offers a serene environment ideal for picnicking and relaxing while enjoying the coastal breeze.
  • Perfect for those looking for a peaceful, less crowded spot away from typical tourist areas.
  • Great for unwinding and appreciating the natural beauty of the coastline.

Jalan Bak-Bak, Kudat 89050 Malaysia

24 Hours

Mantanani Island Beaches


These beaches are postcard-perfect, with powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters. They’re ideal for snorkeling, diving, or simply soaking in the tranquil island vibes.

  • Ideal for snorkeling and diving, offering vibrant marine life and underwater beauty.
  • Perfect for those looking to soak in the tranquil island atmosphere and escape from the hustle and bustle.
