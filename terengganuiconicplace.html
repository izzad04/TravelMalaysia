Istana Maziah


Istana Maziah is an important and historical symbol in Kuala Terengganu, officially opened on 31 May 1895. It was built during the reign of Sultan Zainal Abidin III, showcasing a two-story design influenced by the French architectural style.

  • Opening Date: Officially opened on 31 May 1895, during the reign of Sultan Zainal Abidin III.
  • Architectural Style: The palace features a two-story design with influences from French architecture.
  • Historical Significance: Istana Maziah represents an important symbol of the Terengganu Kingdom's culture and history.
  • Visit Experience: Explore the rich history of the Terengganu Kingdom and enjoy the beautiful gardens surrounding the palace.

Jalan Sultan Zainal Abidin, Kampung Panglima, 20000 Kuala Terengganu

Masjid Kristal


Interested in religious tourism? Please visit the Crystal Mosque! Located in the Islamic Civilization Park area, this mosque features a stunning structure made of steel, glass, and crystal, creating a captivating and unique architecture.

  • Architectural Features: The mosque is constructed using materials such as steel, glass, and crystal, offering a unique and stunning visual experience.
  • Design Highlights: The mosque is known for its majestic dome and towering minaret, contributing to its elegant and captivating appearance.
  • Light Play: Colorful glass decorations on the mosque create a mesmerizing play of light and color, making it even more enchanting.

Pulau Wan Man, Losong Panglima Perang, 21000 Kuala Terengganu

Taman Tamadun Islam


Another religious tourism destination in Kuala Terengganu is the Islamic Civilization Park. This park beautifully combines the charm of nature with the richness of Islamic culture, offering a deep insight into the cultural heritage and civilization of Islam.

  • Replicas of Famous Buildings: The park features 21 replicas of famous Islamic buildings, including the Alhambra Palace, the Old Islamic City of Aleppo, the As-Sakhrah Dome, the Grand Mosque, and the Prophet's Mosque.
  • Cultural Insight: The park provides a unique opportunity to experience the history and beauty of Islamic civilization through its detailed replicas.

Jambatan Angkat Kuala Terengganu


Kuala Terengganu Drawbridge is one of the iconic tourist destinations that must be visited when traveling to Kuala Terengganu. This bridge connects Kuala Terengganu with Kuala Nerus. Its architectural design was inspired by London's Tower Bridge, with two 15-storey towers on the north and south sides.

  • Architectural Design: The bridge's design was inspired by London's Tower Bridge, featuring two 15-storey towers on either side.
  • Panoramic Views: Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the city from the top of the towers.
  • First Lift Bridge: The Kuala Terengganu Drawbridge is the first lift bridge in Southeast Asia.

2000 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu
