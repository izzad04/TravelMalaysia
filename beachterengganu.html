Pantai Batu Buruk


If you're tired of the hustle and bustle of the city, it's time to refresh yourself and relax while enjoying the natural beauty of Batu Buruk Beach! Located in the city of Kuala Terengganu, this beach offers soft sand, clear sea water, and enchanting natural scenery. Here, you can enjoy the soothing sound of crashing waves while savoring delicious seafood. Not just relaxing, you can also experience riding a beautifully decorated horse-drawn carriage around the beach.

  • Relaxing Atmosphere: Enjoy the soothing sound of the waves while relaxing on soft sands and clear sea waters.
  • Delicious Seafood: The beach is a great spot to indulge in fresh, delicious seafood.
  • Horse-Drawn Carriage Ride: Experience a charming ride around the beach on a beautifully decorated carriage.
  • Ideal for Families and Friends: A perfect place for group outings, offering a mix of relaxation and fun activities.

20400, Terengganu

Teluk Mak Nik Beach or Geliga Beach is located in Kemaman, Terengganu. This beach is also known as Monica Bay. There are various facilities available on this beach. It is the first beach you will encounter if you're coming from Kuantan.

Pulau Redang


Located on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia, Pulau Redang is a tropical paradise known for its clear waters and rich marine life. The island offers the best snorkeling experience in Malaysia, making it a must-see destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. A snorkeling trip here allows you to enjoy the underwater beauty, with colorful coral reefs, shoals of tropical fish, and the possibility of encountering sea turtles.

  • Pulau Redang is located on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia and is known for its clear waters and rich marine life.
  • The island offers the best snorkeling experience in Malaysia, attracting nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
  • The calm water and excellent visibility make it easy to explore the marine life of the South China Sea.

Merang Jetty, 21010 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu, Malaysia
