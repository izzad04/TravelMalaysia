Bukit Larut


Bukit Larut (Maxwell Hill) is Malaysia's oldest hill resort, located near Taiping, Perak. Known for its cool climate and lush greenery, it offers a tranquil retreat from the tropical heat. The hill is surrounded by diverse flora, including vibrant flowers and towering trees, making it a paradise for nature lovers.

  • Accessible by 4WD or a challenging uphill hike.
  • Summit provides breathtaking views of Taiping town, its famous Lake Gardens, and the distant Straits of Malacca.
  • Haven for birdwatchers and photographers.
  • Rich biodiversity and misty landscapes.

Bukit Tunggal


Bukit Tunggal is a lesser-known natural attraction located in Perak, Malaysia. This hill offers a serene and refreshing environment for nature enthusiasts and hikers seeking a tranquil escape from the city. Surrounded by lush greenery and scenic landscapes, it provides a peaceful retreat for outdoor activities.

  • Trail to the summit is suitable for hikers of moderate fitness levels.
  • Blend of easy paths and slightly challenging sections.
  • Visitors are rewarded with panoramic views of the surrounding areas, especially during sunrise or sunset.
  • Home to various flora and fauna, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Tanjung Tualang , Kampung Gajah Ipoh Perak


Every day

6:00AM—6:00PM

Bukit Engku Busu


Bukit Engku Busu is a popular hiking destination located in Lumut, Perak, Malaysia. Standing at approximately 331 meters above sea level, it offers a moderately challenging trek suitable for both beginners and seasoned hikers. The hill is named after Engku Busu, a historical figure believed to be a prince from Riau who played a significant role in the region's past.

  • Hike to the summit typically takes about 1 to 2 hours, depending on fitness levels.
  • Visitors are treated to lush greenery, fresh air, and opportunities to spot local wildlife along the trail.
  • At the top, hikers are rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of Lumut town, the Royal Malaysian Navy base, and the nearby coastline.

Bukit Kledang


Bukit Kledang is a popular hiking spot in Ipoh, Perak, offering scenic views and lush greenery. Known for its accessible trails, it caters to both casual walkers and seasoned hikers. The well-paved RTM Trail leads to the summit’s broadcasting tower, while jungle trails provide a more adventurous experience.

  • Famous for its panoramic views of Ipoh, especially during sunrise or sunset.
  • Accessible trails cater to both casual walkers and seasoned hikers.
  • RTM Trail leads to the summit’s broadcasting tower, while jungle trails provide a more adventurous experience.
  • Parking and rest stops are available, making it a favorite destination for locals and visitors alike.

Jalan Kledang, 31450 Ipoh, Perak


Every day

6:00AM—6:00PM
