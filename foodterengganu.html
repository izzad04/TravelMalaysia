Sata - Kak Zah Sata dan Pulut Panggang


Sata can be said to be a food menu that is the main icon of the Kemaman district. There are many shops here that sell sata and it is often a leisurely meal in the evening. Like otak-otak, it is made of fish and wrapped in coconut leaves in the shape of a cone. But it feels different from otak-otak.

  • Sata is a popular food item in the Kemaman district, often enjoyed as a leisurely meal in the evening.
  • Made of fish and wrapped in coconut leaves in the shape of a cone, sata is similar to otak-otak, but with a unique taste.
  • In Kuala Terengganu, delicious sata can be found at Kedai Kak Zah Sata and Pulut Panggang.
  • Kedai Kak Zah is especially loved by locals for its delicious taste, and they also sell grilled glutinous rice.
  • While many shops in Terengganu sell sata, Kak Zah is considered one of the best for its guaranteed deliciousness.

Jln Tengku Ampuan Intan Zaharah, Kampung Batu Rakit, 21020 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu, Malaysia


2:00PM - 6:30PM

Keropok Losong Terengganu


Keropok Losong Terengganu A popular food in Terengganu. There is no need to argue and debate again, the origin of lekor crackers. Since the state is rich in fish resources, there are stalls selling lekor crackers.

  • Keropok Losong is a popular food in Terengganu, especially known for its lekor crackers.
  • Lekor crackers are made from fish and are a traditional delicacy native to Terengganu.
  • One of the must-visit shops is Keropok Warisan Losong, a well-known shop selling lekor crackers.
  • They sell both pre-cooked and frozen crackers, which can be cooked at home.
  • The crackers at Keropok Warisan Losong are known for being delicious, easy to chew, and soft.
  • Don't forget to buy their bottled sauce to complete your lekor cracker meal.

74, Jalan Kuala Hiliran, Kampung Losong Haji Su, 21000 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu, Malaysia


8:00PM - 8:00PM

Ketupat Sotong - Restoran Lempeng Rusila


Ketupat Sotong dish is usually eaten with white rice. There are also some shops that sell this cuttlefish ketupat as a relaxing meal in the evening. In Terengganu, the cuttlefish dish menu is better known as 'tuppat sutong' or 'sutong sumbak'.

  • Ketupat Sotong is a dish traditionally eaten with white rice.
  • In Terengganu, this cuttlefish dish is also known as 'tuppat sutong' or 'sutong sumbak'.
  • It can be found at Restoran Lempeng, where they prepare ketupat sotong with flavorful gravy.
  • The gravy accompanying ketupat sotong is rich and fatty, adding to its delicious taste.
  • A great combination to try at the restaurant is ketupat sotong paired with their specialty, pancakes.

11, Malaysia, Jalan Mawar 3, 21080 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu, Malaysia


9:00PM - 7:00PM

Nasi Dagang - Nasi Dagang atas tol


Nasi Dagang often used as a breakfast food in the State of Terengganu. Traditionally, nasi dagang is wrapped in cone-shaped banana leaves and tied with skewers. This commercial rice is different from others, you know. Usually commercial rice is eaten with cod fish curry and some mixed vegetables.

  • Nasi Dagang is usually served with cod fish curry and mixed vegetables, though customers can also choose chicken as an accompaniment.
  • One of the most popular spots to try nasi dagang in Kuala Terengganu is Nasi Dagang Atas Toll.
  • The food at Nasi Dagang Atas Toll is known for its authentic taste, and it is a favorite among locals.
  • Expect long lines at this restaurant, especially on Sundays and school holidays.

1318, T101, Kampung Pengkalan Setur, 20050 Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu, Malaysia


6:30aM - 12:00PM
