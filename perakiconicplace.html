Kellie's Castle


Kellie's Castle is a historic and unfinished mansion located in Batu Gajah, Perak. Built in the early 20th century by Scottish planter William Kellie Smith, the castle features a unique mix of Moorish, Indian, and Western architectural styles. Although construction was halted due to Smith's sudden death, the castle remains a fascinating site for visitors.

  • Famous for its secret tunnels, offering a glimpse into hidden architectural marvels.
  • Rooftop views provide breathtaking scenery of the surrounding lush greenery.
  • Intriguing legends and ghost stories add an element of mystery to the site.
  • Symbolizes ambition, mystery, and love, making it a must-visit destination in Perak.

Lot 48436, Kompleks Pelancongan Kellie's Castle, KM 5.5, Jalan Gopeng, 31000 Batu Gajah, Perak

Tasik Cermin Ipoh


Tasik Cermin, or the "Mirror Lake", is a hidden gem located in Ipoh, Perak, Malaysia. Nestled within limestone hills, this serene and picturesque lake gets its name from the glass-like reflection of the surrounding cliffs and greenery on its calm, clear waters.

  • Accessed through a short tunnel carved into the limestone, offering visitors a tranquil escape from the city.
  • Perfect for photography enthusiasts, nature lovers, and those seeking a peaceful retreat.
  • Features stunning scenery with dramatic limestone formations and lush vegetation, creating a magical atmosphere.
  • Minimal facilities are available, so visitors are encouraged to respect the environment to preserve its natural charm.

Tasik Cermin, 31350 Ipoh, Perak


Every day

9:00AM—6:00PM

Menara Condong


Menara Condong, or the Tower of Teluk Intan, is an iconic historical landmark located in Teluk Intan, Perak, Malaysia. Built in 1885, this unique structure was originally a water tower and now stands as a symbol of the town’s heritage.

  • The tower leans due to its soft foundation and the weight of the water tank it once housed, earning comparisons to the Leaning Tower of Pisa in Italy.
  • Its striking architecture, resembling a Chinese pagoda, showcases intricate designs reflecting the multicultural influences of the region.
  • At night, the beautifully illuminated tower adds to its charm, making it a popular attraction for photography enthusiasts.
  • Menara Condong remains a proud symbol of Teluk Intan’s rich history and is a must-visit stop for those exploring Perak.

Lot 1&2, Komplex Menara Condong, Jalan Bandar, Pekan Teluk Intan, 36000 Teluk Intan, Perak


Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Saturday, Sunday, and Public Holidays: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Gua Tempurung


Gua Tempurung is one of the largest and most famous limestone caves in Malaysia, located near Gopeng in Perak. Spanning approximately 4.5 kilometers, it offers a stunning natural spectacle with its impressive stalactites, stalagmites, and unique rock formations.

  • The cave features underground streams and intricate chambers that captivate visitors with their beauty and grandeur.
  • Multiple tours of varying lengths and difficulty levels are available, from dry walks to adventurous wet tours involving wading through streams.
  • Equipped with well-lit walkways and informative signboards, Gua Tempurung ensures a convenient and safe exploration experience.

Jln Gua Tempurung, 31600 Gopeng, Perak


Every day

9:00AM—4:30PM
